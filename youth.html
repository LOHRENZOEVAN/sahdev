Youth & Women Empowerment

Building skills and creating opportunities for sustainable livelihoods

Empowering Change Makers

SAHDEV recognizes that women and youth are powerful agents of change in their communities. Our empowerment programs focus on building capabilities, providing resources, and creating spaces for women and youth to lead transformation in their communities.

Skills Training Programs

We offer comprehensive training that prepares participants for sustainable livelihoods:

  • Digital literacy and information technology skills
  • Vocational training in high-demand trades
  • Communication and leadership development
  • Entrepreneurship and business management
  • Financial literacy and access to microfinance

Economic Empowerment Initiatives

We help transform skills into sustainable livelihoods through:

  • Formation and support of savings and lending groups
  • Business incubation and mentorship programs
  • Market access and business networking opportunities
  • Seed capital and equipment grants for startups
  • Ongoing coaching and technical assistance

Women's Rights and Leadership

We work to strengthen women's position in society through:

  • Education on legal rights and advocacy skills
  • Leadership training and mentorship
  • Creating platforms for women's voices in decision-making
  • Addressing gender-based violence and discrimination

Success Stories

Youth Digital Skills Initiative

Our 6-month digital skills program trained 120 young people in web development, digital marketing, and e-commerce. Over 70% of graduates have secured employment or started their own digital businesses, with an average income increase of 150%.

Women's Cooperative for Handicrafts

We helped establish a women's cooperative with 60 members specializing in traditional handicrafts. Through skills training, business development, and market connections, the cooperative now exports products to international markets and has doubled the income of its members.
