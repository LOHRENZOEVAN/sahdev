About Us

Sustainable Action for Humanity and Development (SAHDEV) is a non-profit, apolitical, and inclusive organization based in Cameroon. Our mission is to create a society where every individual has the opportunity to survive, be healthy, grow, and contribute to the socio-economic development of their community.

Our Objectives

  • Eliminate discrimination, social exclusion, hunger, and poverty.
  • Promote peaceful cohabitation of Cameroonian citizens.
  • Encourage skills training for youths and women.
  • Advocate for peace and respect for human rights.
  • Provide relief to vulnerable populations.
  • Promote agriculture value chain development.
  • Promote community development initiatives.
  • Promote environmentally friendly practices.
  • Promote water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) activities.
  • Promote gender equality and health awareness.
