About Us

Sustainable Action for Humanity and Development (SAHDEV) is a non-profit, apolitical, and inclusive organization based in Cameroon. Our mission is to create a society where every individual has the opportunity to survive, thrive, and contribute to the socio-economic development of their community.

Our Vision

We envision a world where men, women, and youth thrive in an environment that fosters good health, economic stability, and social inclusion.

Our Mission

Saving lives, fighting discrimination, hunger, and poverty through sustainable and humanitarian efforts.

Our Objectives

  • Eliminate discrimination and social exclusion
  • Promote peaceful cohabitation
  • Empower youth & women through skills training
  • Advocate for human rights & peace

What We Do

Humanitarian Relief

We provide aid to vulnerable communities affected by conflicts and natural disasters.

Sustainable Agriculture

Supporting farmers with training and resources to enhance production and market access.

Community Development

Improving local infrastructure, education, and healthcare services.

Environmental Conservation

Promoting eco-friendly practices to mitigate climate change and protect biodiversity.

WASH Programs

Ensuring access to clean water and hygiene education for healthier communities.

Gender & Health Awareness

Raising awareness on gender equality, HIV/AIDS prevention, and public health issues.
