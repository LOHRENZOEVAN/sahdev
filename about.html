About SAHDEV

Learn about our mission, values, and the impact we're making

Who We Are

Sustainable Action for Humanity and Development (SAHDEV) is a non-profit, apolitical, and inclusive organization based in Cameroon. SAHDEV was created on October 29, 2021, and registered with the Ministry of Territorial Administration with registration No. 40/E.29/1111/VOL.8/ALPAS by Law No. 90/035 of 19th December 1990. Our mission is to create a society where every individual can survive, be healthy, grow, and contribute to the socio-economic development of their community. We believe in empowering individuals through sustainable practices and humanitarian efforts.

Our Vision

SAHDEV envisions a world where communities are self-sufficient, resilient, and prosperous. We strive for a future where social justice, environmental sustainability, and economic opportunity are accessible to all people, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a society where every individual can survive, be healthy, grow, and contribute to the socio-economic development of their community through sustainable practices and humanitarian efforts that address the root causes of poverty, hunger, and inequality.

Our History

Founded in 2021, SAHDEV emerged from a collective desire to address pressing social and environmental challenges in Cameroon. What began as a small initiative has grown into an organization with a comprehensive approach to sustainable development and humanitarian action.

Our Impact

Since our establishment, we have implemented various initiatives that have positively impacted thousands of lives across Cameroon. Our work spans sustainable agriculture, youth empowerment, environmental conservation, and humanitarian relief efforts.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our activities and interactions.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and ensure that our programs and initiatives are accessible to all individuals, regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, or socioeconomic status.

Sustainability

We are committed to implementing solutions that meet present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Empowerment

We believe in equipping individuals and communities with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to take charge of their own development.
